Interrogating Privacy


Use the map below to filter and discover the COVID apps currently active in your area. Most of these apps came out in 2020 as an effort by the state and national goverments to curb the spread of COVID 19. Maybe you still have some of these on your phone. Maybe you still use them.

Take a moment to look through the permissions you've given to these apps. The handy icon list should help you with that as you click through the map. Or take out your phone and check the permissions of the covid app if it's not present in the list

What data are you currently sharing with your apps? Did you know? How does this make you feel? Does it line up with your comfort levels ? Can we imagine alternatives?

Hold on to these questions as you move through to the next section to discuss with fellow MozFest participants! Add your thoughts to the MIRO Board for this project.
